Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)

We offer Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) services to physician practices in the USA. RCM is a buzzword in the healthcare industry, but what does it really mean?

RCM is the process of managing claims, payment and revenue generation. RCM encompasses everything from determining patient insurance eligibility and collecting co-pays to properly coding claims.

The RCM process begins when a patient schedules an appointment, and it ends when the healthcare provider i.e. doctor has accepted all payments.
